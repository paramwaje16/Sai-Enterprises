## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Statistics Overview
At the top of the dashboard, you'll see:
- **Total Inquiries** - All customer messages
- **Pending** - New inquiries waiting for response (Yellow)
- **Contacted** - Inquiries you've responded to (Blue)
- **Resolved** - Completed inquiries (Green)

### Customer Inquiry Cards
Each inquiry displays:
- ✅ **Customer Name**
- ✅ **Email Address** (clickable to send email)
- ✅ **Phone Number** (clickable to call)
- ✅ **Date & Time** of inquiry
- ✅ **Full Message** from customer
- ✅ **Status Badge** (Pending/Contacted/Resolved)

### Quick Actions
For each inquiry:
1. **Reply via Email** - Opens your email client with customer's email
2. **Call Customer** - Opens phone dialer with customer's number
3. **Refresh** - Get latest inquiries from database

## 📱 How to Use

### Step 1: Access Admin Dashboard
1. Open your web browser
2. Go to your website URL and add `/admin` at the end
3. Example: `https://your-website.com/admin`

### Step 2: View All Messages
- All customer inquiries are listed automatically
- Most recent inquiries appear at the top
- Scroll down to see older messages

### Step 3: Respond to Customers
**Option 1: Reply via Email**
- Click "Reply via Email" button
- Your email client opens with customer's email pre-filled
- Write your response and send

**Option 2: Call Customer**
- Click "Call Customer" button
- Your phone dialer opens with customer's number
- Make the call directly

### Step 4: Refresh for New Messages
- Click the "Refresh" button at the top
- Dashboard updates with any new inquiries
- Statistics update automatically
